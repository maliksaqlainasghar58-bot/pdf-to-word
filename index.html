Why Folium

Conversion that doesn't wreck your formatting.

Most converters flatten your document into a mess of text boxes. Folium rebuilds it as a native, editable Word document.

Pixel-accurate layout

Tables, columns, headers, and page breaks are rebuilt exactly as they appeared in the original PDF — not approximated.

Batch conversion

Drop in up to 50 files at once. Folium queues, converts, and zips them for download while you do something else.

Scanned-document OCR

Photographed or scanned pages are recognized and converted into selectable, editable text automatically.

Font matching

Folium detects the closest licensed system font when the original isn't available, instead of substituting Arial.

Private by default

Every file is encrypted in transit and permanently deleted from our servers within an hour of conversion.

Works everywhere

No plugin, no desktop install. Folium runs entirely in the browser on Windows, macOS, and Linux.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

At Folium, protecting your privacy is one of our highest priorities. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information whenever you use our free online PDF tools. By accessing our website, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

Most of our PDF tools can be used without creating an account. We only collect limited technical information required to operate our services efficiently.

  • Browser type
  • Device information
  • Operating system
  • Anonymous analytics
  • IP address (temporary)
  • Cookies required for website functionality

Uploaded Files

When you upload a document for conversion, compression, merging, or editing, your files are processed securely on our servers. Uploaded files are never sold, shared, or used for advertising purposes.

To protect your privacy, uploaded files are automatically deleted after processing or within a limited period according to our file retention policy.

Cookies

Folium uses cookies to improve website performance, remember preferences, measure traffic, and provide a better user experience.

Google Analytics

We may use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. This information helps us improve speed, usability, and future features.

Google AdSense

Advertisements displayed on our website may be served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to display personalized or non-personalized advertisements based on your browsing behavior.

Users can learn more about Google's advertising policies by visiting Google's Privacy & Terms pages.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption to protect files and personal information during transmission.

  • SSL Encryption
  • Secure File Processing
  • Automatic File Removal
  • No Permanent File Storage
  • Limited Server Access

Third-Party Services

Some services integrated into Folium may be provided by trusted third-party providers including analytics, cloud infrastructure, and advertising networks. These providers have their own privacy policies.

Your Rights

Depending on your country, you may have the right to request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of your personal information.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any modifications will be published on this page with the updated revision date.

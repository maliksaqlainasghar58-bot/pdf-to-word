Quick answers

These questions come up a lot — worth a check before you send a message.

How fast will I get a reply?

Most emails are answered within one business day. Security disclosures and business inquiries may take slightly longer depending on volume.

Can I get help with a specific conversion that failed?

Yes — include the tool you used (e.g. PDF to Word), the approximate file size, and what went wrong, and we'll investigate directly.

Do you offer support for business or bulk use?

Yes. Reach out to our partnerships team for API access, higher file-size limits, or bulk licensing for your organization.
